Skeleton

Skeletons spawn at a light level of 7 or less in groups of 4 or 5 in the Overworld in Java Edition and 1 to 2 in Bedrock Edition. They can be found above solid blocks from all Overworld biomes except mushroom fields. ... Spiders have a 1% chance to spawn with a skeleton riding them, creating a spider jockey.
